Introduction to Study in Fashion Portfolio Checklist

Portfolio Requirements:

Rotation 1: 4-8 pages for each unit

Rotation 2: 4-8 pages for each unit (Design, Business, and Media)

Important: Each unit must begin with a title page that includes your full name, student ID number, and class/group (e.g., 8A).

Important: Your final submission must be a single PDF document that is less than 100MB in size. No other file formats will be accepted.

    Tips

    Research Tips:

    • Your own sketches, photographs, observations

    • Interviews or surveys you've conducted

    Secondary Research (others' work):

    • Magazine/website images (with references)

    • Designer/Artist collections/Research

    • Historical research

  • Creative Text Use: Have you used fonts thoughtfully and creatively?
    Visual Example
    Tips

    How to Download Fonts from DaFont.com:

    1. Visit dafont.com

    2. Search for fonts that match your theme

    3. Click "Download" button next to your chosen font

    4. Extract the ZIP file to your computer

    5. For Windows: Right-click the font file → Install

    6. For Mac: Double-click the font file → "Install Font"

    7. The font will now appear in your design software

    Note: Do not use any Chalkboard based font

  • Source References: Have you properly referenced all your sources?
    Visual Example
    Tips

    Referencing Tips:

    • Include direct references under images or for links include this within your bibliography

    • Format: Artist/Designer (Year) Title

    • Harvard Referencing Guide: https://hsstokes.github.io/Harvard-Referencing-Guide/

    • For print sources: Author (Year) Title. Publisher, Place of publication, p.##
